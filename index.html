About Me

Hi, I'm Rob McIntosh, an information technology professional with a passion for data and web technologies. With several years of experience in the field, I have developed a strong expertise in managing and analyzing complex data sets, leveraging technology to drive insights and improve decision-making processes. I thrive in the fast-paced and ever-evolving world of IT, constantly seeking innovative solutions to enhance operational efficiency and drive business growth.
In addition to my work in IT, I am also a college hockey coach for my alma mater. Hockey has always been a huge part of my life, and it's been incredibly rewarding to be able to combine my love for the sport with my expertise in data analysis. By using data to inform our coaching decisions and strategy, we've been able to achieve some great results on the ice. Overall, I feel incredibly fortunate to be able to pursue both of my passions in my career, and I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow in both the technology and hockey worlds.
